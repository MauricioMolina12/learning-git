Brown Rice

is a whole grain rice variety that retains its bran and germ layers, making it more nutritious than white rice. It has a nutty flavor and a chewy texture compared to the softer and milder taste of white rice. The outer layers of bran and germ in brown rice contain valuable nutrients such as fiber, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.





Meat

Is a popular dish in Mexican and Latin American cuisine. In English, "carne asada" translates to "grilled or roasted meat." It typically refers to thinly sliced beef, usually skirt steak or flank steak, that has been marinated and then grilled or cooked on an open flame. The marinade often includes ingredients like lime or orange juice, garlic, onions, and various spices to enhance the flavor and tenderness of the meat.
